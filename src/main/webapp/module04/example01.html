Sending cookies

Cookies will be saved on your local machine, and the format will depend on each browser type. If you are using Chrome, you can view all the cookies executing the following sequence:

  • From the menu in the top right corner of the browser, select Settings.
  • At the bottom of the page, click Show advanced settings
  • Under Privacy, select Content settings
  • Click All cookies and site data
  • Click on the host that sent the cookie (use localhost if you are using this example locally)
You are encouraged to inform your users that you are using cookies.
